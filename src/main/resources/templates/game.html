Bulls and Cows Game

Description: The computer guesses a 4-digit number. The digits of the secret number are not repeated.
Your task is to guess the that number. You have an unlimited number of attempts.
In each attempt, you give the computer your version of the number.
The computer reports how many digits are correctly guessed (bull)
and how many digits are guessed without regard to position (cow).
Based on the answer of the computer, you have to guess the number in several steps..

Statistics: Average number of attempts to win - 20

Enter your number. Try to guess!
